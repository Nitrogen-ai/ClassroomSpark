About

Behind ClassroomSpark

👨‍🏫

Made by a Teacher, for Teachers

ClassroomSpark is a personal project born from real classroom needs. Every tool is designed with pedagogical intent — thoughtful learning experiences that help students practise, make mistakes, and improve.

The apps are crafted iteratively: conceived, tested, and refined until they work the way a good exercise should. They'll always be free to use. If you find them useful, a small tip helps keep the project going.

☕ Support ClassroomSpark

These tools are free because education shouldn't have a paywall. If an app saved you preparation time or helped your students — a small tip goes a long way.

Legal

Terms of Use

Usage Rights

All tools on ClassroomSpark are provided free of charge for personal and educational use. You may download, use, and share them with colleagues and students. You may adapt them for your own classroom needs.

Restrictions

You may not resell these tools or claim them as your own creation. You may not redistribute modified versions under a different brand without attribution. Commercial use beyond individual classroom settings requires prior written consent.

Tip-Based Model

ClassroomSpark operates on an honesty-based tip model. There is no obligation to pay, and all tools remain fully functional without payment. Tips are voluntary contributions to support ongoing development.

Disclaimer

Tools are provided "as is" without warranty. While great care is taken to ensure accuracy, no guarantee is made. Content may be updated or corrected at any time.

Privacy

ClassroomSpark tools run entirely in your browser. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored on any server. Your students' information stays on your device.
